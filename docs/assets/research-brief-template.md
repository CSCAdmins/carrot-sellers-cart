# Person Research Brief Template

Use this prompt with Claude web or other research tools to gather comprehensive information for a new Carrot Sellers profile.

---

## The Prompt

Copy and customize the text below:

```
I need comprehensive, well-sourced information on [PERSON NAME] for a UFO/UAP credibility tracking website. Please research and provide:

## 1. BACKGROUND & CREDENTIALS
- Full career history with dates
- Relevant credentials (military, government, academic, journalistic)
- Education and training
- Awards/recognition in their field
- Public profile before entering UAP discourse

## 2. ENTRY INTO UAP DISCOURSE
- When did they first make public UAP-related statements?
- What prompted their involvement?
- Initial claims or positions

## 3. TIMELINE OF KEY STATEMENTS
For each major public statement, provide:
- Exact date
- Platform (hearing, interview, podcast, book, social media)
- Key claims made (verbatim quotes preferred)
- Source URL or citation

Cover all significant appearances from their first UAP statement through present.

## 4. CLAIMS INVENTORY
List each distinct factual claim they've made about:
- UFO/UAP sightings or encounters
- Government programs or cover-ups
- Crash retrievals or materials
- Non-human intelligence
- Insider knowledge or sources

For each claim:
- Date first made
- Corroborated by others? (who?)
- Verified, debunked, or unverified?

## 5. FACT-CHECK STATUS
- What has been independently verified?
- What has been officially disputed or debunked?
- What remains unverified?
- Official responses (Pentagon, DoD, NASA, etc.)

## 6. CREDIBILITY ASSESSMENT DATA
Help me score these criteria (0.0 = no issues, 1.0 = major problems):

**Evidence Quality:**
- Do they cite firsthand or secondhand sources?
- Do they provide verifiable evidence?
- Do they rely on anonymous sources?

**Logical Consistency:**
- Have their positions changed over time?
- Any contradictions in their statements?
- Do they acknowledge uncertainties?

**Transparency:**
- Do they cite specific sources?
- Have they corrected errors publicly?
- Are they clear about what they know vs. believe?

**Sensationalism:**
- Measured or dramatic language?
- "Bombshell" framing or cautious presentation?
- Proportionality of claims to evidence?

**Monetization/Ethics:**
- Books, documentaries, speaking fees?
- Patreon, merchandise, paid content?
- Disclosed conflicts of interest?
- Commercial relationships with UAP industry?

## 7. SUPPORTERS & CRITICS
- Who publicly supports their claims? (names, credentials)
- Who has criticized or disputed them? (names, credentials)
- Notable endorsements or condemnations

## 8. RED FLAGS & POSITIVE INDICATORS
- Any documented errors or retractions?
- Legal issues or credibility controversies?
- Track record in their field before UAP involvement
- Peer recognition or awards

## 9. SOURCES
Provide URLs for:
- Primary sources (their own statements, testimony, books)
- Major media coverage
- Fact-check articles
- Official government responses
- Critical analyses

Prioritize:
- Primary sources over commentary
- Archived links when available
- Date range: [RELEVANT YEARS]

## OUTPUT FORMAT
Please organize findings clearly with:
- Dates for all events
- Direct quotes where possible
- Source citations for each claim
- Clear distinction between verified facts and unverified claims
```

---

## After Research: Mapping to Profile

Use research findings to complete:

| Research Section | Maps To |
|------------------|---------|
| Background | Title, Known For, Platform fields |
| Timeline | Quote dates and sources |
| Claims Inventory | Quote content and fact-checks |
| Credibility Assessment | 5 scores + rationale notes |
| Red Flags/Positives | Additional context sections |
| Sources | Sources section at bottom |

---

## Scoring Cheat Sheet

| Score | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 0.0-0.2 | Excellent | Peer-reviewed sources, corrections made, no conflicts |
| 0.3-0.4 | Good | Minor issues, mostly transparent |
| 0.5-0.6 | Mixed | Some concerns, inconsistencies |
| 0.7-0.8 | Problematic | Multiple red flags, unverified claims |
| 0.9-1.0 | Major issues | Debunked claims, hidden conflicts, maximum hype |

---

## Quality Checklist

Before creating profile:

- [ ] At least 5 distinct sources consulted
- [ ] All quotes are verbatim with dates
- [ ] Each score has documented rationale
- [ ] Both supportive and critical perspectives included
- [ ] Primary sources prioritized over commentary
- [ ] All URLs verified working
- [ ] Red flags AND positive indicators noted
