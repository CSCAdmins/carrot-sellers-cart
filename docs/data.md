### ðŸ“– Data Dictionary

#### People Data Fields
- **Name**: Individual's name as commonly known
- **Title**: Professional title or role
- **Known For**: What they're primarily recognized for in UFO/UAP space
- **Active Since**: Year they became prominent in UFO/UAP discourse
- **Primary Platform**: Main channels where they publish/speak
- **Affiliations**: Organizations or groups they're associated with
- **Evidence Score**: Quality of sources cited (0.0-1.0, more = worse)
- **Consistency Score**: Logical consistency over time (0.0-1.0, more = worse)
- **Transparency Score**: Willingness to cite sources/admit errors (0.0-1.0, more = worse)
- **Sensationalism Score**: Use of dramatic language (0.0-1.0, more = worse)
- **Monetization Score**: Ethical monetization practices (0.0-1.0, more = worse)
- **Total Score**: Sum of all category scores (0.0-5.0, more carrots = more BS)
- **Category**: Overall rating (Highly Credible â†’ Maximum Carrot Seller)

#### Quote Data Fields
- **Person Name**: Who said it
- **Quote Date**: When it was said
- **Source**: Where it was published/broadcast
- **Quote Text**: Exact verbatim quote
- **Fact Check Status**: TRUE/FALSE/UNVERIFIED
- **Person Total Score**: That person's overall carrot rating
- **Person Category**: That person's overall credibility category
