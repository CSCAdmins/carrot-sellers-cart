## Our Mission

The Carrot Sellers tracker exists to bring transparency and accountability to the UFO/UAP discourse. In a field often clouded by speculation, misinformation, and sensationalism, we aim to:

- Document claims accurately and in context
- Track the reliability of prominent voices
- Provide fact-checking where possible
- Maintain an objective, evidence-based approach

## Why "Carrot Sellers"?

The name reflects our purpose: identifying who's "selling carrots" (peddling unsubstantiated claims) versus providing genuine insights into the UFO/UAP phenomenon. The more carrots someone has, the more BS they're selling.

## Methodology

### Inclusion Criteria
We track individuals who:
- Have significant public platforms
- Make specific, verifiable claims about UFOs/UAPs
- Influence public discourse on the topic
- Present themselves as authorities or insiders

### Quote Selection
We document:
- Direct quotes only (no paraphrasing)
- Specific claims that can be evaluated
- Predictions or announcements
- Major policy or factual assertions

### Fact-Checking Process
Each claim is evaluated as:
- **TRUE**: Independently verified
- **FALSE**: Demonstrably incorrect
- **UNVERIFIED**: Cannot be confirmed or denied with available information

### Rating Process

1. **Initial Assessment**: Each person is evaluated against all five criteria
2. **Quarterly Reviews**: Scores are updated based on new content and behavior
3. **Peer Review**: Multiple reviewers assess each person to ensure consistency
4. **Documentation**: All scoring decisions are documented with specific examples
5. **Appeals Process**: Individuals can request re-evaluation with new evidence

## Editorial Guidelines

1. **Objectivity**: We focus on documenting statements, not attacking individuals
2. **Accuracy**: All quotes must be verbatim with proper sourcing
3. **Context**: We provide relevant background for fair interpretation
4. **Updates**: We track corrections and clarifications
5. **Transparency**: Our methods and criteria are public

## Contributing

We welcome contributions that improve the accuracy and completeness of our tracking:

### Quick Contributions
- **Quote submissions**: Submit documented quotes with sources via PR
- **Corrections**: Fix errors in existing information
- **Fact-checking**: Verify or update claim statuses
- **Source links**: Add or fix broken source links

### Major Contributions (Discussion Required)
- **New people**: Must open GitHub Issue first for community discussion (minimum 7 days)
- **Score changes**: Provide evidence for score re-evaluation via Issue
- **Methodology updates**: Propose changes to our 5-criteria system

### Quality Standards
- All quotes must be verbatim with verifiable sources
- Sources must be publicly accessible
- New people must demonstrate significant influence in UFO/UAP discourse
- Scoring must follow our established 5-criteria methodology
