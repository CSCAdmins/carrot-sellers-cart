## Background

**Career**: Senior Special Correspondent for NewsNation (November 2023-present). Hosts "Reality Check with Ross Coulthart." Previously 60 Minutes Australia, Four Corners (ABC), Sunday (Nine Network, 14 years), Sunday Night (Seven Network). Over three decades at major Australian outlets.

**Education**: Law Degree, Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand.

**Awards**:
- **Gold Walkley Award (2008)** — Australia's highest journalism prize ("Butcher of Bega" investigation)
- **5 Walkley Awards** — Total career wins (Australia's equivalent to Pulitzer)
- **Logie Award (1996)** — Most Outstanding Achievement in Public Affairs
- **Prime Minister's Prize for Australian History (2015)** — Charles Bean biography
- **New York Film Festival Gold Medal (2002)** — East Timor war crimes investigation
- **Law Council of Australia Journalist Award** — Inaugural winner

**Controversy**: In 2018, worked as PR for Ben Roberts-Smith's firm Cato and Clegg. Commissioned by Seven West Media to investigate war crimes allegations; reportedly claimed evidence would vindicate Roberts-Smith. Federal Court (June 2023) later found Roberts-Smith participated in murders of Afghan civilians.

## Key Investigations

### David Grusch Interview (June 2023)
- Exclusive NewsNation interview that launched Grusch into prominence
- Claims: covert crash retrieval program, "non-human origin technical vehicles," recovered "biologics"
- Directly triggered July 26, 2023 Congressional hearing
- Coordinated with Leslie Kean, Ralph Blumenthal; rejected by NYT, WaPo, The Hill, Politico before The Debrief published

### Tic Tac / Lockheed Martin Claims (July 2025)
- Claimed USS Nimitz "Tic Tac" is "categorically" Lockheed Martin technology
- Claims craft operated "neuromeditatively" (mind control)
- Contradicts years of his own reporting implying non-human origin
- Commander David Fravor has pushed back

### Luis Elizondo Coverage
- Multiple NewsNation interviews with former AATIP director
- Covered Elizondo's 2024 book "Imminent"
- Covered January 2025 letter to President Trump urging disclosure
