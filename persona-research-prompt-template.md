# UFO/UAP Persona Research Template

## Research Request for: [PERSON NAME]

I need comprehensive research on [PERSON NAME] for a fact-checking website that evaluates UFO/UAP claims. Please provide:

### 1. Basic Information
- Full name and any aliases/nicknames
- Current title/role
- What they're primarily known for in the UFO/UAP community
- When they became active in UFO/UAP topics
- Primary platforms (books, podcasts, TV, social media, etc.)
- Current and past affiliations (organizations, companies, government agencies)

### 2. Notable Claims & Positions
- List their most significant UFO/UAP claims
- Include direct quotes with dates and sources
- Note any evolution or changes in their positions over time
- Identify their core narrative or message

### 3. Evidence & Credibility Assessment
For each major claim, evaluate:
- What evidence do they provide?
- Can the evidence be independently verified?
- Do they cite sources?
- Have any claims been definitively proven true or false?

### 4. Scoring Analysis (0.0 = best, 1.0 = worst)
Provide scores and justification for:
- **Evidence Quality**: How well-supported are their claims?
- **Logical Consistency**: Do they maintain consistent positions?
- **Transparency**: Do they cite sources and admit errors?
- **Sensationalism**: Dramatic claims vs measured analysis?
- **Monetization Ethics**: How do they profit from their claims?

### 5. Key Quotes
Find 3-5 significant quotes that represent their positions, including:
- Exact quote text
- Date and source
- Context (what prompted the statement)
- Fact-check status (TRUE/FALSE/UNVERIFIED)
- Brief explanation of why it's true/false/unverified

### 6. Red Flags or Positive Indicators
- Any history of proven false claims
- Transparency about mistakes or retractions
- Financial interests or conflicts of interest
- Professional credentials relevant to claims
- Peer recognition or criticism

### 7. Sources
Provide links to:
- Primary sources (their own content)
- Secondary sources (articles about them)
- Fact-checks or debunking attempts
- Archive links where available

Focus on factual, verifiable information rather than personal attacks. The goal is fair, evidence-based evaluation of their UFO/UAP claims and credibility.
